The FAOSTAT Population module contains time series data on population, by sex and urban/rural. The series consist of both estimates and projections for different periods as available from the original sources, namely:
  • Population data refers to the World Population Prospects: The 2015 Revision from the UN Population Division.
  • Urban/rural population data refers to the World Urbanization Prospects: The 2014 Revision from the UN Population Division.
