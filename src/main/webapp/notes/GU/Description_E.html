Manure applied to Soils


Greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions from manure applied to soils consist of nitrous oxide gas from nitrogen additions to managed soils from treated manure. Computed at Tier 1 following the 2006 IPCC Guidelines for National Greenhouse Gas Inventories; available by country, with global coverage and relative to the period 1961 to present, with annual updates, and projections for 2030 and 2050.
