Greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions data from grassland are currently limited to emissions from grassland organic soils. They are those associated with carbon losses from drained histosols under grassland . Data is computed at Tier 1 and complemented by use of geo-spatial data, following the 2006 IPCC Guidelines for National GHG Inventories. Available by country, with global coverage and relative to the period 1990 to present.
