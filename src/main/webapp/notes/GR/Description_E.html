Rice Cultivation


Greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions from rice cultivation consist of methane gas from the anaerobic decomposition of organic matter in paddy fields. Computed at Tier 1 following the Revised 1996 IPCC Guidelines for National GHG Inventories and the IPCC 2000 Good Practice Guidance and Uncertainty Management in National GHG Inventories; available by country, with global coverage and relative to the period 1961 to present, with annual updates, and projections for 2030 and 2050.
