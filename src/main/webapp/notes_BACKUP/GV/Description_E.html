Cultivation of Organic Soils


Greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions data from cultivation of organic soils are those associated with nitrous oxide gas emissions from drained histosols under cropland and grassland. Data is computed at Tier 1 and complemented by geo-spatial data, following the 2006 IPCC Guidelines for National GHG Inventories. Available by country, with global coverage and relative to the period 1990 to present and projections for 2030 and 2050.
