Cropland


Greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions data from cropland are currently limited to emissions from cropland organic soils. They are those associated with carbon losses from drained histosols under cropland. Data is computed at Tier 1 and complemented by use of geo-spatial data, following the 2006 IPCC Guidelines for National GHG Inventories. Available by country, with global coverage and relative to the period 1990 to present.
